Increased demand on logistical activities such as the movement of medicines, specimens, and clinical supplies around a hospital is leading to delays to care in hospitals across the UK. By automating certain tasks, we can free up time for hospital staff to do the jobs that people do best.

THIS IS WHY WE INVENTED HELPER BOTS

INTELLIGENT HELPER SOLUTIONS

Drawing on our work in developing self-driving cars for the road, our Helper Bots use advanced vision technology originally developed for self-driving cars to navigate in complex environments.

Originally developed for use in hospitals, these Helper Bots can also be used in restaurants, cafes, offices or other busy indoor environments where you need to move things from A-B.

Carry items autonomously from A-B securely

Can be customised for different logistics needs

Can speak multiple languages
